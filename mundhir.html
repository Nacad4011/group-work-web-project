The Economic Power of Zanzibar Tourism

How Tourism Drives Profits

Zanzibar is one of Africa’s top travel destinations. Tourism is the largest contributor to the archipelago's economy[cite: 3].

Key Profit Areas:

  • Employment: Over 30,000 direct jobs created in the hospitality sector[cite: 26].
  • Infrastructure: Tourism revenue pays for modernizing airports and roads[cite: 26].
  • Local Trade: Resorts purchase seafood and spices directly from local villages[cite: 26].
