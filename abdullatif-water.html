The Essence of Life: Water

Water

The Foundation of Everything

Why Water Matters

Water is the most essential resource on Earth. It covers about 71% of our planet's surface and makes up approximately 60% of the human body. Without it, life as we know it would cease to exist.

The Hydrologic Cycle

Nature has a perfect recycling system. Through evaporation, condensation, and precipitation, water moves from the oceans to the sky and back to the land, sustaining ecosystems along the way.

Conservation

Even though our planet is "blue," only a tiny fraction of our water is drinkable. Protecting our waterways from pollution and reducing waste is vital for future generations.
